Create a Great Listing

Here are six ways to set yourself up for success.

Write a standout title

  • Use search terms buyers often use.
  • Avoid all caps and focus on details like brand, model, size, and color.

Take high-quality photos

  • Capture items in well-lit places with multiple angles.
  • Use apps to clean up images and add white backgrounds.

Pick a purchase format

  • "Buy It Now" is great for quick sales.
  • Use "Auction" for competitive pricing.

Set the right price

  • Use recent sales data for pricing.
  • Enable "Best Offer" for negotiation.

Stand out from the crowd

  • Use sponsored placements for visibility.
  • Utilize advertising tools for faster sales.

Ship with ease

  • Follow delivery recommendations.
  • Offer local pickup for nearby buyers.
